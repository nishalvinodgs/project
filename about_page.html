Our Story

Discover the journey behind thriftIN and our passion for sustainable fashion

Our Beginnings

Founded in 2020, thriftIN began as a small boutique in Mumbai with a simple mission: to make premium fashion accessible while promoting sustainability.

Our founders, fashion enthusiasts with a vision, recognized the growing need for a platform where high-quality pre-owned items could find new homes, reducing waste in the fashion industry.

What started as a passion project quickly grew into India's leading marketplace for premium pre-owned fashion, with thousands of satisfied customers across the country.

Our Philosophy

At thriftIN, we believe that fashion should be both luxurious and responsible. We're committed to changing the way people think about pre-owned items.

Every piece in our collection undergoes a rigorous authentication process to ensure quality and authenticity. We're proud to offer items that are not only beautiful but also have a story to tell.

By choosing pre-owned, our customers become part of a movement that values craftsmanship, reduces environmental impact, and makes luxury accessible.
